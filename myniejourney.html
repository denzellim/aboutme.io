My Journey

National Institute of Education

NIE Year 1

In my first year of NIE, I have grasped the concepts and skills of teaching, managing and understanding the Primary Level students through General Education modules. I experienced learning how to teach music differently and more effective through the use of the Experience-Concept-Apply (ECA) approach, which I would adopt in my future lessons. I learnt to work on my singing and performing skills, ICT tools such as GarageBand which will allows me to enhance my teaching in the music classroom.

NIE Year 2

In my second year, I was introduced and given the opportunity to learn and perform various instruments of a Pop Band, such as the bass guitar, keyboard and drum set. I had gained insights of what World Music is, and how important it is to be exposed to music around the world, and not be held within the comfort zone of the Western Classical Music. I learnt how I could introduce Western Classical Music through the EuroAmerican Art tradition, where I could make relevant to students' experience and daily lives. These 2 years of experience and education in NIE has groomed me to be a better musician, educator and person. I hope to inspire students to learn and enjoy music making.
